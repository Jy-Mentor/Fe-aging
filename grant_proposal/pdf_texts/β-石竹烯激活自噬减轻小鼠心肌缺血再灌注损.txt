β-石竹烯激活自噬减轻小鼠心肌缺血再灌注损伤的
作用及机制
周智华1，杜琼颖2，郅青3
【摘要】目的 研究β-石竹烯激活自噬减轻小鼠心肌缺血再灌注损伤的作用及机制。方法 选择
雄性C57BL/6J小鼠，随机分为假手术组、模型组、β-石竹烯组、3-甲基腺嘌呤（3-MA）组、β-石竹
烯+3-MA组共五组，采用左前降支结扎的方法建立心肌缺血再灌注模型，造模前给予β-石竹烯灌胃、
3-MA腹腔注射。检测心电图改变，血清乳酸脱氢酶（LDH）、磷酸肌酸激酶同工酶（CK-MB）含量，
心肌梗死面积及病理改变，自噬小体数目及Beclin-1、微管相关蛋白1轻链3（LC3）表达水平。结果 模
型组ST段、血清LDH及CK-MB含量、心肌梗死面积、心肌中自噬小体数目及Beclin-1、LC3-II/LC3-I表
达水平均高于假手术组（P＜0.05）；β-石竹烯组ST段、血清LDH及CK-MB含量、心肌梗死面积均低于
模型组，心肌中自噬小体数目及Beclin-1、LC3-II/LC3-I表达水平均高于模型组（P＜0.05）；β-石竹
烯+3-MA组ST段、血清LDH及CK-MB含量、心肌梗死面积均高于β-石竹烯组，心肌中自噬小体数目及
Beclin-1、LC3-II/LC3-I表达水平均低于β-石竹烯组（P＜0.05）。结论 β-石竹烯能够减轻小鼠心肌再
灌注损伤，激活自噬是可能的分子机制。

Effect and mechanism of β-caryophyllene on reducing myocardial ischemia-reperfusion injury by
activating autophagy in mice Zhou Zhihua*, Du Qiongying, Zhi Qing. *Department of Cardiology, No. 988
Hospital of the Joint Logistics Support Force of the People's Liberation Army Zhengzhou, 450000 Zhengzhou, China
Corresponding author: Zhou Zhihua, Email: 2384777923@qq.com
[Abstract] Objective To study the effect and mechanism of β-caryophyllene on reducing myocardial
ischemia-reperfusion injury by activating autophagy in mice. Methods Male C57BL/6J mice were randomly
divided into sham group, model group, β-caryophyllene group, 3-methyladenine (3-mA) group and
β-caryophyllene+3-mA group. The myocardial ischemia-reperfusion model was established by ligation of left
anterior descending coronary artery. Before modeling, β-caryophyllene was given by intragastric administration
and 3-mA was intraperitoneally injected. The ECG changes, contents of lactate dehydrogenase (LDH) and
creatine kinase isoenzyme (CK-MB) in serum, myocardial infarction area and pathological changes, the number of
autophagosome and the expression levels of beclin-1 and microtubule-associated protein 1 light chain3(LC3) were
detected. Results The ST segment, contents of LDH and CK-MB in serum, myocardial infarct size, the number of
autophagosome and the expression levels of beclin-1 and LC3-II/LC3-I in model group were higher than those
in sham group (P＜0.05); the ST segment, contents of LDH and CK-MB in serum, myocardial infarct size of
β-caryophyllene group were lower than those in model group, the number of autophagosome and the expression
levels of beclin-1 and LC3-II/LC3-I were higher than those in the model group (P＜0.05); the ST segment, contents
of LDH and CK-MB in serum, myocardial infarct size of β-caryophyllene+3-MA group were higher than those
ofβ-caryophyllene group, the number of autophagosome and the expression levels of beclin-1 and LC3-II/LC3-I
were lower than those of β-caryophyllene group (P＜0.05). Conclusion β-caryophyllene can reduce myocardial
reperfusion injury in mice, and activation of autophagy is a possible molecular mechanism.
